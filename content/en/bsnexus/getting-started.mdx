**AI handles the work. You make the decisions.**

BSNexus is the command layer for AI-native companies. The first vertical slice is the AI-native software company: you give product direction, AI handles planning, design, implementation, testing, and reporting, and BSNexus returns decisions, briefs, and verified deliverables.

You should not need to sit at a computer to keep company work moving. You direct, decide, and review.

## The Loop

| Step | What you do | What BSNexus returns |
|------|-------------|----------------------|
| **Direct** | Give a short direction in natural language. | A request, scoped to a project, with the next concrete step. |
| **Decide** | Resolve only the choices AI should not make alone — taste, scope, risk, budget, deploy approval. | Resumed work and an updated brief. |
| **Review** | Inspect what shipped. | A deliverable with proof you can verify. |

Tactical choices (frameworks, file paths, default conventions) are decided by AI. You only see the questions that actually need founder judgment.

## Quick Start

### Step 1 — Sign in

1. Go to [nexus.bsvibe.dev](https://nexus.bsvibe.dev).
2. Click **Sign in with BSVibe**. If you don't have a BSVibe account yet, sign up at [auth.bsvibe.dev](https://auth.bsvibe.dev).
3. After signing in, you land on the dashboard: decisions that need you, the latest brief, shipped work, and active projects.

### Step 2 — Create a project

1. Click **+ New Project** in the top right of the dashboard.
2. Enter a project name (e.g. `todo-app`, `blog-platform`).
3. Optionally describe what the project is about. This becomes the founder context for every direction you give later.

### Step 3 — Give direction

Open the project and write what you want next, in one or two sentences:

> "Build a small to-do API in FastAPI with JWT-based auth and Postgres. Add a smoke test and confirm it boots."

> "Update the marketing page so the headline matches our new positioning. Keep the tone short and operator-like."

> "Investigate why the deploy preview is failing and fix the underlying cause."

BSNexus turns each direction into a Request, decomposes it into internal execution runs, and starts work. You do not assign agents, draft prompts, or design a board.

### Step 4 — Resolve decisions

When AI hits a fork that should not be decided alone, it raises a Decision in your inbox: a short title, the question, the recommendation, the options, and what each option costs. Resolve it from anywhere — web or mobile.

Decisions are the only place BSNexus interrupts you. Anything that has a sensible default is handled silently.

### Step 5 — Review verified deliverables

When the work is done, BSNexus returns a deliverable with **proof**:

- the verification command that was run
- the exit code and a short summary
- artifact links — PR, preview URL, screenshot, or test result
- known risks and limitations

If proof is missing or verification failed, BSNexus does not claim the work is shipped. You can reject, redirect, or approve.
