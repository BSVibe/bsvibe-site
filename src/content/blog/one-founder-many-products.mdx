To build several products at once, you can't sit and watch any one of them. You need to know "what is everything doing right now" in five seconds.

BSVibe's **Brief** answers that.

## Sentences, not charts

No token counts, no round counts, no cost graphs. Instead:

- bsvibe-site · writing tests for the related-posts feature · 4m in
- nexus-core · needs a decision from you
- data-pipeline · shipped · view PR

In plain language, the way a person would tell you.

## It calls you only when needed

Most work just flows. Only genuine forks — taste, scope, direction — bring you in. The rest of the time, it's quiet.

That's the difference from a management console. BSVibe isn't built to be watched. It's built so you don't have to.
