Most AI tools finish a task and hand you the result. One PR, one doc. Then you have to review all of it yourself.

BSVibe is different. Every result comes with a **Delivery Report**: the intent, what was built, how it was checked, the verdict, and the artifact. One page.

## "Done" is not proof

An LLM is strong at making things and weak at reviewing what it made. So review can't rest on the model's own say-so.

BSVibe's verification is mechanical. It actually runs the tests, actually builds, and shows the result. Not "it says it passed" but "it passed — here's the log."

## Proof a human can read

Not engineer-only terminal output. It shows what was intended, what changed, and why it can be trusted, in plain language.

With proof, trust stops being a leap of faith and becomes a result. That's where BSVibe starts.
