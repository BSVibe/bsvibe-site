Delegating to AI is exhausting for one simple reason: **you fix the same thing over and over.** It breaks a convention you taught it yesterday, and re-asks a direction you set last week.

BSVibe remembers your corrections.

## Correct it once, and it steers around it

Every time you fix a result, make a decision, or reframe a direction, that judgment accumulates. When the same signals appear in later work, BSVibe applies your standard on its own.

Supervision once is enough. So the work you have to touch **shrinks** over time.

## This is the one promise

The only honest way to say "delegate and walk away" is for mistakes to stop recurring. Not a static config file, but a memory that grows the way you work. That's why BSVibe is different.
